EMPLOYEE WELFARE


Like many successful companies, Megalon firmly believes that a company is only as good as its employees. We believe that a positive work environment can increase employee productivity and contribute to innovation and overall company success. Hence, employee welfare is always our company’s top priority.

Megalon provides amenities such as a health clinic, a prayer site, a cafeteria and sport facilities on the factory’s premises to ensure that our workers have a healthy and conducive working environment. We hope to foster a sense of belonging in our workers and this is the key in delivering quality products and service excellence to our customers.





COMMUNITY SERVICE


Megalon’s social responsibility does not end at its main gates. The company also recognizes that it has a certain role to play in its surrounding community in which it operates.

Over the years, Megalon has actively participates in various community affairs, including sponsoring sport events and contributing towards the construction of religious and civil buildings. We continuously seek to participate in improving the local communities we work with, and provide assistance during times of need. Ultimately, Megalon strives to be a holistic company that plays a vital role in shaping the future of our society.
