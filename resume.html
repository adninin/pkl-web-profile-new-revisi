Resume


FIRST FACTORY

1980

P.T Megah Megalon Industries was established in Indonesia as a manufacturer of interlinings. Our first factory was located in Cibinong covering a total area of 11000 square meters with 200 employees. Our marketing office was located at Jalan Pangeran Jayakarta No. 73 Blok E 1/7 Jakarta 10730, Indonesia.

1984

Our Cibinong factory expanded over the years and added 2 lines of non-woven machineries, totaling our production lines for non-woven to 3.

1992

Our first line of paste-dot coating machinery and another line of micro-dot coating machinery were added.

1993

We purchased and installed our first 4 units of powder grinding machineries to facilitate our coating processes.

SECOND FACTORY

1997

The Cileungsi factory was established, occupying a total area of 70000 square meters with 100 employees and 1 line of non-woven machinery. By this time, our Cibinong plant had added 6 additional powder grinding machineries




2000

One line of cutting-edge non-woven machinery from Germany was installed in Cileungsi plant.

RELOCATED

2003

The Cibinong plant was closed down as all the non-woven machineries were outdated and not operational sustainable. At the same time, our marketing office was relocated to Wisma Megah Lt. 4 Jalan Danau Sunter Utara Blok N-2 No.2-3 Jakarta 14350, Indonesia.

UPDATED

2006

Our Cileungsi plant added an additional line of non-woven machinery and we consolidated all our production capabilities to produce micro-dot woven and non-woven interlinings here.
