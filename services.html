QUALITY CONTROL


Megalon upholds its commitment to quality products and service excellence by adherence to strict quality control. This is done with stringent check on raw materials and the manufacturing processes. In particular, three areas are emphasized :


  • Continuous improvement in all aspects of production
  • Cutting edge technologies are implemented throughout the process
  • Choice materials are outsourced from reputable suppliers





    • GLOBAL DEMAND




    Megalon’s growing export is a testimony to the company’s competitive strength in the global market. In 2010 alone, Megalon produced and sold more than 80 million yards of interlinings, of which 40% were exported abroad. Megalon’s growing international market today compromises of the Middle East, East Europe, Africa and ASEAN countries.

    A 70% share of the domestic market clearly establishes Megalon as the market leader in Indonesia, where its successful export venture since 1989 underlines the company’s main strength in product quality, coupled with consistency in on-time delivery and competitive value.






    INNOVATION




    Megalon’s consistent expansion in its range of products is to meet the growing needs of applications for interlinings. At present, we have more than 50 types of interlinings in our woven and non-woven range.

    Megalon’s interlinings find their way to an amazingly diverse array of products. Most of them go to the garment industries, where they are used as extra lining to the outer material or regular lining in various kinds of garments. Our interlinings have also been applied in making shoes, hats, bags and wallets as well.

    Product innovation through research and development is a never-ending quest for Megalon due to the growing demand of product applications that require advanced, high-quality interlinings. These efforts, along with technological advances that open up new vistas in the use of interlinings, will no doubt add to Megalon’s expanding product lines.
